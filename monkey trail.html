ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Monkey Trail is a fun and interactive walking path where visitors can observe playful monkeys in a natural, open-style environment. The trail is designed to give you a close-up view of these energetic and intelligent animals.

As you walk along the trail, you’ll see monkeys swinging through trees, playing together, and exploring their habitat. Educational signs along the path provide interesting facts about different monkey species and their behaviour.

Monkey Trail is perfect for families and animal lovers. Take your time, enjoy the scenery, and don’t forget to bring your camera for some great photo opportunities.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find Monkey Trail near the rainforest section, close to the primate enclosure. Follow the signs from the main path.

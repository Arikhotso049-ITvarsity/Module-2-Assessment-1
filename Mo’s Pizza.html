MO’S PIZZA

Mo’s Pizza is a family-friendly restaurant known for serving delicious, freshly made pizzas using quality ingredients and traditional recipes. Our dough is prepared fresh daily, our sauces are rich and flavorful, and our toppings are always generous.

We offer a wide variety of pizzas including classic favourites like Pepperoni and Margherita, as well as specialty pizzas such as Meat Lovers, BBQ Chicken, and Veggie Deluxe. In addition to pizza, we also serve sides, wings, salads, and refreshing drinks.

At Mo’s Pizza, customer satisfaction is our priority. Whether you choose to dine in, take away, or order for delivery, we aim to give you a warm, welcoming experience and great food every time.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND US

You will find Mo’s Pizza in the food court area, near the main entrance. Look out for the smell of freshly baked pizza!

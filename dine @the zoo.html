DINE @ THE ZOO

Dine @ The Zoo is a unique dining experience where visitors can enjoy delicious meals while surrounded by the sights and sounds of the zoo. It offers a relaxed and family-friendly atmosphere for all ages.

The restaurant serves a variety of meals including burgers, pizzas, sandwiches, salads, and kid-friendly options. Refreshing drinks and tasty desserts are also available.

Whether you are taking a break from exploring or planning a sit-down meal with family and friends, Dine @ The Zoo is the perfect place to relax and refuel.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find Dine @ The Zoo near the central plaza, close to the lion enclosure and main rest area.

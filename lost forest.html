LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a peaceful and mysterious area filled with tall trees, winding paths, and the sounds of nature all around you. It is the perfect place to escape the busy parts of the park and enjoy a calm, natural environment.

As you explore the Lost Forest, you may encounter a variety of plant life and small animals that call this forest home. Shaded walkways and hidden corners make it an exciting place for visitors who enjoy adventure and discovery.

The Lost Forest is ideal for nature walks, photography, and quiet reflection. It offers a cool, relaxing atmosphere, especially on warm days.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Lost Forest on the eastern side of the park, just beyond the lake and close to the nature trail.

INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is an exciting indoor exhibit where visitors can discover the fascinating world of insects. It showcases a variety of species including butterflies, beetles, ants, and stick insects in carefully designed habitats.

Learn how insects live, grow, and survive through informative displays and interactive exhibits. The Insect House helps visitors understand the important role insects play in nature and the environment.

This attraction is perfect for curious minds of all ages. Whether you are interested in science or just want to see something unique, the Insect House offers a fun and educational experience.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Insect House near the botanical gardens, close to the education centre. Follow the signs along the main pathway.

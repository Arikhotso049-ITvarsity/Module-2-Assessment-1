WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

Wild Things Coffee Shop is a cozy and relaxing spot where visitors can take a break from exploring the zoo and enjoy freshly brewed coffee and tasty treats in a peaceful setting.

The coffee shop offers a variety of hot and cold drinks including cappuccinos, lattes, hot chocolate, and iced coffee. A selection of pastries, muffins, sandwiches, and light snacks is also available.

Whether you need a quick caffeine boost or a quiet place to relax, Wild Things Coffee Shop is the perfect stop for all coffee lovers.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find Wild Things Coffee Shop near the bird enclosure, close to the shaded seating area.
